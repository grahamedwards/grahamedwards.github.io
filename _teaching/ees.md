Introductory Earth science course with an emphasis on Earth's systems and environmental geoscience.

### Topics Covered
Systems, plate tectonics, rock cycle, geologic hazards, water &amp; water resources. 

### Course structure
- Lecture and activity-based classroom sessions.
- 2 take-home midterm assessments
- Field trip to geologic sites in the San Antonio area.
- Final geo-photography project

### Semesters taught
Spring 2025, planned Spring 2026
